About Sangeetam

"Sangeetam 2023," the eagerly awaited annual cultural extravaganza of Shobhit Deemed University, goes beyond a mere celebration of art and culture. It embodies unity, diversity, and limitless creativity. With the theme "Harmonizing Cultures, Amplifying Talents," the festival signifies a dedication to fostering mutual respect, cross-cultural understanding, and the celebration of multifaceted talents. Its core objective is to weave a vibrant tapestry of cultures, offering a dynamic platform for students to express their identities, showcase their talents, and nurture innovative ideas.

This event is more than a festival; it's an opportunity for everyone to find belonging amidst the rich mosaic of cultures that enrich the university. The diverse program includes performances, art exhibitions, interactive sessions, fashion showcases, entrepreneurial insights, literary evenings, and a star-studded finale, aiming to unify, deepen understanding, and encourage collaboration among students.

Ultimately, "Sangeetam 2023" reflects the university's commitment to a thriving, harmonious, and culturally enriched community, inviting all to join in celebrating the beauty of diverse cultures and talents.

FAQ's

What is Sangeetam?

Sangeetam is a cultural fest organized by Shobhit University, Meerut, annually. At the heart of Sangeetam 2023 lies the objective of creating a vibrant tapestry of cultures where every thread contributes to the beauty of the whole. Our aim is to provide a dynamic platform for students to express their unique cultural identities, showcase their artistic abilities, and nurture innovative ideas. The event is not just a festival; it's an opportunity for everyone to find a sense of belonging and inclusivity amidst the rich mosaic of cultures that enrich our university.

When and where is Sangeetam taking place this year?

Sangeetam is taking place at Shobhit University Meerut on December 8 and 9, 2023.

Will there be any opportunities for networking and socializing at the event?

From stalls of companies to youth from multiple colleges, the event will create high exposure opportunities.

What is the target audience for the event?

College and school students

How can we connect with the team?

Feel free to reach us out through our official email address: sangeetam@shobhituniversity.ac.in

Are there any specific rules or guidelines for attendees to follow?

Discipline and proper decorum should be maintained throughout the event. And all event rules and regulations you can find in the event posts on our social media.

What are the events happening at Sangeetam?

Go through the event page of the website.

How do I register for the events at Sangeetam?

You can register for your interested game or competition through the website and choose “Register as a Guest” if you are not a student of Shobhit University, fill out the required fields asked on CollPoll, and then pay the amount of your respective event.

Will there be accommodation available for participants and guests of Sangeetam?

No, there is no accommodation facility will be provided to stay at Shobhit University. You can find so many hotels and restrooms near our Campus to stay.
